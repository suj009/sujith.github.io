About Me


Sujith was born in Bangalore, India and completed his Bachelors in Information Science and Engineering. Then he moved to Newcastle Upon Tyne, United Kingdom for pursing first MSc studies in Advanced Computer Science. In his first master thesis, he was introduced to the field of User Centered Design or Participatory Design. The work he practiced and delivered in his thesis eventually led him to pursue another master studies in Human Computer Science. Apart from his academics, he also did his master thesis and first internship in Microsoft, UK. Later, he worked in a start up called Emocial Ltd in London, UK as a UX Designer and did some freelance work for non-profit organization before moving to Stuttgart, Germany for another internship as a UX Research and Designer at IBM Studios, Böblingen.
