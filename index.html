About Me


I was born in Bangalore, India and completed my Bachelors education in Information Science and Engineering. Then, I moved to Newcastle Upon Tyne, United Kingdom for pursing first master programme in Advanced Computer Science at Newcastle University. During first master thesis, I was introduced to the field of User Centered Design. The work I practiced and delivered in that thesis eventually led me to pursue another master studies in Human Computer Science. Apart from academics, I also did master thesis and first internship in Microsoft, UK. Later, worked in a startup, Emocial Ltd in London, UK as a UX Designer. Then, worked as a freelancer UX Designer for a non-profit organization before moving to Stuttgart, Germany for another internship as a UX Research and Designer at IBM Studios, Böblingen.
